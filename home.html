Welcome to WebFit

At WebFit, our team has designed a simple, user-friendly way to achieve your workout goals—together. Forget downloading endless gym apps or paying hundreds of dollars a year just to face cross-platform issues between Android and iPhone. We’ve simplified fitness for you with one easy-to-use website where you can create custom workouts, learn proper form, and share your routines with friends and family effortlessly. No more app clutter, no more hassle—just real fitness, made simple. Let’s get fit, with WebFit.

- Instructions -
The easiest way to understand how to create an exercise is to watch the video on this page!
To get started on a workout, head over to the Exercise Page or click the Get Started link below.

1. Select the Muscle Group(s) that you would like to exercise.
2. Look through the list of exercises provided and select the ones you would like to perform. (Demonstration videos are provided for help).
3. Add the selected exercises to your workout.
4. Choose your Sets/Reps
5. Get Fit!
Feel free to share your workout with friends with the 'share' button on the exercise page
